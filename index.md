## <span id="about"></span> About me

I&nbsp;am a Staff Research Scientist at [Meta](https://tech.facebook.com/reality-labs/) (2020–2023 in Seattle, from 2023 in Zurich), working on spatial AI for AR/VR and robotics. My current research focuses on vision foundation models for 3D vision, particularly zero-shot object detection and pose estimation, as well as language-grounded spatial understanding. I created and maintain the [BOP benchmark](https://bop.felk.cvut.cz/), which became the standard for evaluating object pose estimation, and organize [workshops](http://cmp.felk.cvut.cz/sixd/workshop_2024/) at major vision conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV). Prior to joining Meta in 2020, I received my PhD in computer vision under the supervision of [Prof. Jiri Matas](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=EJCNY6QAAAAJ) at the [Czech Technical University in Prague](https://fel.cvut.cz/), during which I interned at [Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-redmond/) and [Google](http://www.stefan-hinterstoisser.com/). I serve as an Area Chair at CVPR and ECCV.
